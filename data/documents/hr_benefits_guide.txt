EMPLOYEE BENEFITS GUIDE
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. HEALTH INSURANCE

The company provides comprehensive health coverage to all full-time employees and their eligible dependents, effective from the first day of employment.

Medical Insurance: Employees may choose from three plan tiers — Basic, Standard, and Premium. The company covers 80% of the employee-only premium for all tiers. Employees selecting family coverage are responsible for the difference above the employee-only subsidy. Deductibles range from $500 (Premium) to $2,000 (Basic) per individual per year.

Dental Insurance: The company covers 100% of preventive dental care (cleanings, X-rays) and 80% of basic restorative procedures (fillings, extractions). Orthodontic coverage is included in the Premium plan only, at 50% up to a $2,000 lifetime maximum.

Vision Insurance: All employees receive an annual eye exam covered at 100%, plus a $200 annual allowance toward frames, lenses, or contact lenses.

Open Enrollment: Employees may review and change their health plan elections each year during the Open Enrollment period in November. Life events such as marriage, birth, or divorce allow mid-year changes within 30 days of the event.

2. RETIREMENT PLAN (401k)

The company offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan administered through Vanguard. Employees are eligible to enroll from their first day of employment.

Company Match: The company matches 100% of the first 4% of employee contributions, up to a maximum company contribution of $6,000 per year. The match is vested immediately — it belongs to you from day one.

Contribution Limits: Employees may contribute up to the IRS annual limit ($23,000 for 2024; $30,500 for employees aged 50 and over). Contributions are made pre-tax, reducing your taxable income.

Investment Options: Employees may choose from a range of Vanguard index funds, target-date funds, and fixed income options. A default target-date fund based on your expected retirement year is selected if no election is made.

To enroll or change your contribution rate, log in to the Vanguard portal at vanguard.com/company.

3. LIFE AND DISABILITY INSURANCE

Basic Life Insurance: The company provides group life insurance equal to 2x the employee's annual base salary at no cost to the employee. Employees may purchase additional voluntary life insurance at group rates for themselves, a spouse, or dependent children.

Short-Term Disability (STD): Employees who are unable to work due to illness or injury are eligible for STD benefits after a 7-day waiting period. STD pays 60% of base salary for up to 12 weeks.

Long-Term Disability (LTD): If disability extends beyond 12 weeks, LTD benefits provide 60% of base salary until the employee is able to return to work, reaches age 65, or the maximum benefit period is reached.

4. REMOTE WORK AND HOME OFFICE STIPEND

The company supports flexible working arrangements. Employees approved for remote work receive a one-time home office setup stipend of $1,000 to cover equipment and ergonomic needs. Receipts must be submitted within 90 days of approval.

Employees working remotely full-time also receive a monthly internet stipend of $75, paid through payroll as a non-taxable reimbursement.

5. PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

The company is committed to supporting employee growth and learning. Each employee receives an annual professional development budget of $2,000, which may be used for:

  - Online courses, certifications, or bootcamps
  - Industry conferences or workshops (including travel)
  - Books, subscriptions to learning platforms (e.g., Coursera, Pluralsight)
  - Professional association memberships

Unused professional development budget does not roll over to the following year. Requests for expenses over $500 require advance manager approval. Submit receipts through the expense portal within 30 days of the expense.

6. WELLNESS BENEFITS

Gym Membership Reimbursement: The company reimburses up to $600 per year (up to $50 per month) toward gym membership, fitness classes, or sports club fees. Submit receipts through the HR portal monthly.

Employee Assistance Program (EAP): All employees and their household members have access to free, confidential counseling and support services through the company's EAP provider. Services include up to 8 free therapy sessions per issue per year, financial counseling, and legal consultations. Contact the EAP directly at 1-800-EAP-HELP (available 24/7).
